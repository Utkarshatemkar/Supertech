About SuperTech

Who We Are

SuperTech is a leading instrumentation and electrical contracting company specializing in automation solutions for process industries. With decades of experience, we’ve built a reputation for delivering reliable, scalable, and cost-effective systems tailored to the unique needs of our clients.

Our expertise spans across Paints, Resins, Pharmaceuticals, Chemicals, Agrochemicals, and more. We integrate advanced technologies like PLCs, SCADA, DCS, MES, and ERP to help industries achieve operational excellence. From design and engineering to installation and commissioning, SuperTech is your trusted partner in industrial automation.

Our Vision

To be the most preferred Automation Solution Provider in a wide range of Process Industries.

Our Mission

To deliver reliable, efficient, and innovative automation systems that empower industries to achieve operational excellence and sustainability.

Our Company Journey

SuperTech began its journey with a vision to transform industrial automation across India. Over the years, we’ve partnered with leading manufacturers in Paints, Chemicals, Pharmaceuticals, and more — delivering smart solutions that integrate PLCs, SCADA, MES, and ERP systems.

From small-scale installations to large turnkey projects, our team has consistently pushed boundaries in automation, instrumentation, and electrical contracting. Today, SuperTech stands as a trusted name in the industry, known for quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Our journey is defined by our commitment to excellence, our passion for technology, and our dedication to helping industries evolve through intelligent automation.
